About Markas Enterprise MKE

Markas Enterprise MKE is the heart-child of Ochieng' Mark Oketch — a dog lover, tech enthusiast, storyteller, and community builder. Built from passion and purpose, our enterprise blends love for animals, creativity, and digital innovation to create a meaningful experience for both pets and people.

🐾 Our Mission

To provide exceptional pet care, quality products, and education while fostering a community of responsible pet ownership

💡 What We Do

  • Maintain a safe and joyful kennel environment for dogs
  • Offer a curated pet store with essentials and accessories
  • Run a bookstore with heartwarming tales and real-life dog adventures
  • Share insights, experiences, and updates through our blog
  • Promote adoption, awareness, and responsible pet parenting

🌍 Our Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted pet care brand — where dogs, people, and innovation thrive in harmony. We aim to inspire connection, kindness, and community impact in everything we do.

📍 Where We're Based

Markas Enterprise MKE is proudly based in Kenya, operating locally but thinking globally.
